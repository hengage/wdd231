Simple Weekly Plan

  • Monday: Learn 5 new words and 2 example sentences.
  • Tuesday: Review old words and listen to short audio.
  • Wednesday: Practice introductions and common questions.
  • Thursday: Revisit difficult words and retake quiz.
  • Friday: Write 5 short German-English phrase pairs.
  • Weekend: Light review and consistency check.
